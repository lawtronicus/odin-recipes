Mint Julep

What is a Mint Julep?

The Mint Julep is the classier (and, frankly, tastier) version of the Mojito. It is refreshing and minty, and is famous as the drink most-enjoyed by attendees of the Kentucky Derby.

Throw on a pastel shirt, bowtie, white linen blazer, and a panama hat, and let's get to the races!

Ingredients

  • Eight to twelve mint leaves
  • Simple syrup (1/4 oz.)
  • Bourbon (2 oz.)
  • Mint sprig (garnish)

Steps

  1. Lightly muddle mint leaves and simple syrup in a Julep cup or rocks glass.
  2. Add bourbon.
  3. Pack glass tightly with ice.
  4. Stir until cup is frosted along the outside.
  5. Top with more crushed ice to create an ice dome.
  6. Give the mint sprig a quick slap to release the oils and vapors, and place in the ice dome.
  7. Enjoy!
